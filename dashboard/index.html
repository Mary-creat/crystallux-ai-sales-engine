          What's tracked & what isn't
          

            Tracked: Crystallux outreach (emails, calls via Vapi), appointments held, follow-ups, leads qualified / deals closed, dashboard session time (aggregate).

            NOT tracked: browsing outside Crystallux, keyboard/mouse, screen recording, personal messages, activities outside work hours, non-Crystallux apps.

            Data retention: 12 months then anonymised. Withdraw consent anytime via support. See docs/operations/PRODUCTIVITY_TRACKING_CONSENT.md.
