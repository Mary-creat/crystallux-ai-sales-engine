The math

An SDR agency vs. an AI sales engine: the real math

7 min read

When you decide it is time to get serious about new business, you have three real options: hire someone in-house to do outreach, pay an agency to do it, or run an AI sales engine. Here is the honest comparison, including the parts the agencies do not put on their slides.

What each one actually costs

OptionTypical costWho writes the outreach
In-house rep$8,000 to $12,000 a month, loadedYou train them, then they do
Outreach agency$3,000 to $5,000 a monthOften still you, or a junior
AI sales engineFrom a few hundred a monthThe engine, in your voice

The sticker price is only half the story. An in-house hire comes with recruiting, training, payroll, benefits, and the very real chance they leave in a year and you start over. An agency comes with a setup fee, a ramp-up period, and the awkward truth that you are often the one still writing the messages they send.

What each one actually gets you

An in-house rep gives you a real human who knows your business, but they can only work so many hours, and outreach is repetitive work that burns people out fast.

An agency gives you a team and a process, but you are one of many clients, the people on your account change, and the copy often reads like it came from a template, because it did.

An AI sales engine works around the clock, never gets tired of follow-ups, writes a fresh message for every lead, and costs a fraction of a single salary. What it does not do is replace the human judgment in a sales call, which is exactly where you want to be spending your time anyway.

Where each one wins

If you have complex, high-touch deals that need a senior person managing every relationship, a great in-house hire is worth it. If you want to fully outsource and never think about it, an agency can work, if you find a good one.

But if what you want is a steady flow of qualified meetings on your calendar, at a price that does not need a board meeting to approve, the engine wins on nearly every line: cost, consistency, speed, and the quality of each individual message.

The honest bottom line

You do not have to choose forever. Plenty of operators run the engine to fill the top of the funnel and keep a human for the closing. The point is that the old default, pay thousands a month and still do half the work yourself, is no longer the only option. That is the whole reason Crystallux exists.
