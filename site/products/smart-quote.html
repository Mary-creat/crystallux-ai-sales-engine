How it works

From customer click to booked job in four steps. Your onboarding specialist tunes the questions, rules, and add-ons per industry. The engine handles the rest.

Customer fills the form

Industry-specific questionnaire, 5-6 questions. Modern UI, mobile-first, saves drafts.

Engine computes the price

Your pricing rules and add-ons are applied securely on our side. The final price reflects your business, not a customer's guess.

Email + quote page deliver

The email lands instantly. The quote page is shareable with one link. Your customer accepts or declines in one click.

Follow-up + conversion

Accept books the job. Decline stops the follow-ups. No response? It gently nurtures them over 14 days.
