How it works

From customer click to booked job in four steps. Mary tunes the questions, rules, and addons per industry. The engine handles the rest.

Customer fills the form

Industry-specific questionnaire, 5-6 questions. Modern UI, mobile-first, saves drafts.

Engine computes the price

Pricing rules + addons + tier-picking logic run server-side. Final price reflects your business logic, not a customer's guess.

Email + quote page deliver

Postmark email lands instantly. Public quote URL is shareable. Customer accepts or declines with one click.

Follow-up + conversion

Accept triggers booking creation. Decline stops follow-up. No response? Cron nurtures over 14 days.
