What it does

The Sales Engine runs your whole sales motion. Every step is tracked, every decision is logged, and every message goes out signed by you.

🗺️

Finds local businesses

Searches by city, industry, and the kind of customer you want, then sorts them so the best-fit businesses rise to the top.

🔍

Researches each one

Email, phone, company size, buying signals and more. Every lead lands in your pipeline already researched, so you never start cold.

📈

Scores and prioritises

Warmer leads get a personal touch like a call or a video. Cooler ones go into a gentle long-term nurture. Nothing slips through.

✉️

Reaches out six ways

Email, text, WhatsApp, LinkedIn, calls, and personal videos. It picks the right channel for each person automatically.

🔁

Handles the replies

It reads every reply. Interested? It books the call. Not yet? It follows up at the right time. Unsubscribes are honoured instantly.

📅

Books the meeting

The meeting lands on your calendar, and you get a short briefing beforehand so you walk into every call prepared.

How it works

From your input to booked meetings. You give it your industries, your cities, and your pricing. It does the rest.

Tell it who you sell to, which cities to cover, what to say, and how often to reach out. A one-time setup with our help.

It finds people

It sorts them

Every lead gets a warmth score based on real buying signals, so the best opportunities always rise to the top.

It reaches out and closes

Outreach across every channel, replies handled, meetings booked, and a short summary in your inbox each day.
