What it does

The Sales Engine is the full operating system. Every phase is a workflow you can audit, every decision is logged, every message is signed by you.

🗺️

City-scan discovery

Pull businesses from Google Maps by city + industry + product type. WS6 routes per lead_type (carrier_prospect / advisor_candidate / smb).

🔍

Apollo enrichment

Email, phone, employee count, tech stack, intent signals, behavioral archetype. Every lead arrives in your pipeline pre-researched.

📈

Lead scoring + signal detection

Buy signals + intent score + behavioral fingerprint. Hot leads route to voice + video. Cold leads go to long-tail nurture.

✉️

Six outbound channels

Email, SMS, WhatsApp, LinkedIn, voice calls, and personalised AI videos. Channel choice driven by score, signal, and lead preference.

🔁

Reply ingestion + routing

Inbound replies parsed by Claude. Positive intent triggers booking flow. Objections route to the right follow-up. Unsubscribes honored instantly.

📅

Booking automation

Calendly link or direct booking. Pre-meeting briefing AI walks you into every call prepared. Post-call analyzer scores the outcome.
