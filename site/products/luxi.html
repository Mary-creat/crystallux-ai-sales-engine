What it does

LUXI is the live-commerce equivalent of an auctioneer, a card terminal, and a customer list rolled into one. One auction per item, across every platform, with no manual matching of payments.

🎤

Multi-platform live

One auction broadcasts to TikTok Live, Instagram Live, Facebook Live, and YouTube simultaneously, with an AI co-host that can answer questions live.

💬

Comment-to-bid parsing

Bid parser watches comments across every platform. Identifies bid amount + bidder handle. Registers new bidders automatically and recognises them across every platform.

⏱️

Anti-snipe extension

Bid lands within the anti-snipe window? Auto-extends the close by configurable seconds. Caps at max-extensions to prevent runaway auctions.

💳

Stripe capture on win

The winning bid is charged automatically, and holds on everyone who was outbid release on their own. No manual matching, no chargebacks from confused bidders.

🖥️

Manual bid entry

Some bids come from off-platform DMs or in-person buyers. Admin page lets you enter manual bids during the live, attributed correctly.

📊

Post-auction analytics

Engagement per platform, bid velocity, winner profile, conversion funnel. Refine the next auction with the previous one's data.

How it works

Configure once, run unlimited auctions. The platform handles broadcast, bid capture, anti-snipe, payment, fulfillment notification.

Create the auction

Item details, starting bid, increment, anti-snipe window. From the LUXI admin in under 60 seconds.

Go live

Stream feeds to every platform. Bid parser starts watching comments. Manual bid entry is one click if a bid lands off-platform.

Auction extends + closes

Anti-snipe extensions handled automatically. When the timer hits zero, winner is locked in. Stripe captures their hold.

Fulfill

Winner notified by email + DM. Shipping label triggered if integrated. Outbid holds released. Next auction can start.
