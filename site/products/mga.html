How it works

From advisor application to commission deposit, the MGA module runs the full workflow. Compliance officers, supervisors, and the principal each get their role-appropriate view.

Advisor applies

Public form captures licensing + carrier preferences + product lines. Lands in the principal's review queue.

Verify + onboard

E&O verification, KYC, license check, 30-day curriculum. Compliance score computed from the data collected.

Place business

Application builder + final review + carrier submission. Suitability interview locked in. Commission expectation recorded.

Reconcile + report

Carrier statements match expected commissions. Monthly reports to advisors, the principal, and carriers through their branded portals.
