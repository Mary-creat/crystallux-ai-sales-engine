How it works

Four steps from contract to live monitoring. Sentinel ships as a managed service on your existing infrastructure — no rip-and-replace.

Discovery call (30 min)

We map your current ops stack: workflows, crons, vendors, alerting today, what wakes you up. Identify the highest-pain monitoring gap.

Deploy + integrate (1 week)

Sentinel agents connect to your n8n, Airflow, Cron, k8s, cloud provider — wherever your workflows run. Read-only at first. We baseline normal behavior.

Tune + activate (1 week)

Calibrate which alerts wake you vs which Sentinel handles. Connect runbooks. Set escalation policies. Turn on self-healing for known patterns.

Live + iterate

Daily briefings start. Cost anomalies surface. Sentinel learns your operation. Monthly review tunes thresholds based on actual signal-to-noise.
