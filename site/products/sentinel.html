What it does

Sentinel runs in the background. It watches, diagnoses, fixes the routine, and escalates only what genuinely needs you.

🛡️

24/7 monitoring

Every cron, webhook, scheduled job. If something stops firing, you know before customers notice.

🚨

Tier-1 triage

Filters false positives, runs known runbooks, only wakes you on genuine sev-1 incidents.

💸

Cost anomaly detection

7-day rolling baseline per workflow. A 3x spike in API costs hits your inbox in an hour, not when the invoice arrives.

📋

Daily briefing

One paragraph at 08:00 local: what fired overnight, what was healed, what needs you today. Written like a senior engineer handing off shift.

🔧

Self-healing

Common failure patterns (stuck workflow, backed-up queue, drifting DB connection, expiring cert) trigger autonomous remediation.

📊

Audit-ready log

Every alert, every action, every escalation logged immutably. Compliance review is a 10-minute export.
