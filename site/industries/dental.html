Dental FAQ

What dental practices ask first.

Is the messaging RCDSO-compliant?

Yes. Templates avoid outcome claims, diagnostic claims, comparative advertising against named practices, and testimonials referencing specific treatments without consent. Reviewed against RCDSO Ontario (strictest), with parallel compliance maps for ADA Alberta, CDSBC BC, and OdQ Quebec.

What practice-management systems do you integrate with?

Dentrix, OpenDental, and ClearDent integrations are included in the founding tier. Other PMS systems can be added via the bespoke integration add-on ($1,500 one-time).

How do you handle no-shows?

SMS confirmation 24 hours before consult, plus a same-day reminder. Tier A clients get the no-show recovery sub-system that re-engages a missed prospect with a rebook flow.
