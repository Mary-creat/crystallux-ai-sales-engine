About

Built in Toronto for Canadian operators.

Crystallux was started by Mary Akintunde to solve the same pipeline problem she watched her network struggle with year after year: the sales engine only moves when you personally move it.

The founder

Mary Akintunde is a Canadian licensed insurance professional based in Toronto. She spent years watching fellow operators (brokers, contractors, consultants, dentists) burn their weekends on cold outreach and still hit month-long dry spells. The outreach tools they tried were templated, sounded like marketing departments, and converted at 1 to 3 percent. The SDR agencies they hired charged $4,500 per month and fired their proposals at the same ICP with no niche tuning.

Mary built Crystallux to be the thing she wished existed: a universal sales engine that reads like a peer in your trade, handles the end-to-end pipeline, and ships with a money-back guarantee so operators are not the ones eating the risk.

The thesis

Every Canadian SMB operator we have talked to has the same complaint: the pipeline only moves when you personally move it. Weekends disappear into proposals. Business development eats the hours that should go to delivery. And the outreach that actually works (peer-to-peer, specific, human) does not scale past 10 leads a week.

Our AI engine can write peer-advisor outreach better than any templated tool. Combine that with built-in lead intelligence, multi-channel delivery, and per-vertical overlays, and you get a platform that replaces the SDR agency and the in-house BDR at a fraction of either cost.

A founding insurance broker rated our cold outreach 9.5 out of 10 and said it was the first cold outreach that sounded like a peer, not a vendor. That is the bar.

Canadian by design

  • Based in Toronto. Crystallux Inc. is incorporated in Ontario with a Canadian bank and a physical business address.
  • CASL-compliant outreach. Every message carries sender identification, business address, and a working unsubscribe.
  • PIPEDA-aligned data handling. Canadian privacy law governs how we handle client and lead data.
  • Billed in CAD. GST and HST auto-calculated per province.
  • Vertical compliance review. RIBO for insurance, RCDSO for dental, RECO for real estate, LSO for legal (when activated).
  • DNCL compliance. Voice outreach gated on a real CRTC lookup before activation.

The platform

Crystallux runs on production-grade cloud infrastructure with row-level security, an AI-first automation layer, AI-authored outreach, built-in lead intelligence, and managed billing. The dashboard is built on the same codebase as the engine, with enforced client isolation and role-based views for admin, client, and ops users.

The engine is universal. The overlays are vertical-specific. The infrastructure is production-grade. The team is small and relentless on unit economics.
