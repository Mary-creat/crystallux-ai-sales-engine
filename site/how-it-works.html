How it works

From signing up to a full pipeline in 21 days.

Here is exactly what happens after you sign up. No mystery, no "it depends." A clear timeline, with a guarantee at the end.

Book a demo See pricing →
1
Day 0

Sign up and onboarding call

Sign the agreement, then a 30-minute call the same day or the next. We capture who your best customers are, which channels to use, your calendar link, and any leads you want to keep.

What you bring: your calendar link, a business email for sending, and one sentence on your top three customer types.
2
Day 1 to 3

We find your first prospects

We load the first 50 prospects that match your ideal customer, then research each one overnight: a short summary and the single most relevant reason they might buy.

What you see: your dashboard is live with 50 researched leads. Review them, flag any that are off, and we tighten the filter.
3
Day 4 to 7

First outreach goes out

The engine writes the first batch of personal outreach: emails always, plus any channels you turned on. You review the first 10 to 15, flag anything off, we tune it, and it sends.

An early broker rated our cold outreach 9.5 out of 10 and said it was the first that sounded like a peer, not a vendor. That is the bar.
4
Day 7 to 14

Replies and follow-ups

Interested replies land on your calendar and in your inbox. The engine follows up on its own on day 3 and day 7 with anyone who did not reply. You only step in for the actual conversation.

5
Day 14

First checkpoint call

A 15-minute call: your wins, the numbers, anything in the way, and one improvement for the week ahead. Weekly through month three, then every two weeks.

6
Day 21

Full pipeline running

By the end of week three you have a steady daily flow of outreach, qualified meetings booking onto your calendar, and a reliable rhythm. The guarantee runs through day 30: if we have not hit your target, your next month is free.

Built around your industry

You are not getting a generic tool with a setting flipped. You are getting outreach that reads like someone in your trade wrote it.
