How it works

From contract to full pipeline in 21 days.

Here is exactly what happens after you sign. No mystery. No "it depends." A clear timeline with a guarantee at the end.

Day 0. Contract and onboarding call

Sign the agreement. 30-minute onboarding call that same day or the next. We capture your ICP (top 3 customer types, size, service area, vertical), channel preferences (email always on; LinkedIn, SMS, voice, video opt-in), Calendly URL, sender-email config, and any existing leads you want preserved.

What you bring

Your Calendly link, access to a business email for outreach delivery, and a 1-sentence description of your top 3 customer types.

Day 1 to 3. Prospect sourcing

We load the first 50 prospects matching your ICP using built-in lead intelligence and vertical-specific intent signals. Lead research runs overnight: our AI engine drafts a one-paragraph summary and the most relevant pain signal for each prospect.

What you see

Your dashboard URL is live. 50 researched leads waiting. Review them, flag any off-target ones, and we refine the filter.

Day 4 to 7. First outreach

Our AI engine generates the first batch of personalised outreach: emails (always), plus any channels you have activated. You review the first 10 to 15 drafts. Flag anything that feels off. We tune the prompt. Once approved, the platform sends.

Peer-reviewed copy

A founding insurance broker rated our cold outreach 9.5 out of 10 and said it was the first one that sounded like a peer, not a vendor. That is the bar.

Day 7 to 14. Replies and follow-ups

Qualified replies flow into your Calendly and notification email. The platform runs day-3 and day-7 follow-ups automatically on non-responders. You only step in at the conversation stage. No time on copy, cadence, or follow-up timing.

Day 14. First checkpoint call

15-minute call. Wins, metrics review, blockers, one optimisation for the next week. Weekly cadence continues through month 3, then scales to bi-weekly.

Day 21. Full pipeline running

By the end of week 3, you have a consistent daily outreach flow, qualified meetings booking on your calendar, and a reliable week-over-week cadence. The guarantee window runs through day 30. If we have not hit your vertical's target by then, your next month is free.

Universal engine, vertical-specific depth

The Crystallux engine is one platform. Every vertical has its own overlay: pain-signal taxonomy, outreach tone, compliance constraints (RIBO for insurance, RCDSO for dental, LSO for legal), preferred channel mix, and pricing. You are not getting a generic SaaS tool tuned with a config flag. You are getting copy that reads like a peer in your trade wrote it.
