The problem

Pipeline is the work that takes you away from the work.

Three reasons service businesses lose hours every week.

1

You're stuck being the rainmaker

You spend evenings writing proposals instead of delivering. Lead generation eats hours that should go to your customers. The pipeline only moves when you personally move it.

2

Your outreach doesn't sound like you

Templated emails. Generic LinkedIn messages. The outreach that actually works (peer-to-peer, specific, human) doesn't scale past 10 leads a week.

3

Your stack is held together with tape

CRM here. Sequencer there. LinkedIn in a tab. Calendly somewhere else. You're the integration layer. One missed handoff and a customer walks.
