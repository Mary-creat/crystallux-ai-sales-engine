Common pricing questions

What does "locked for 12 months" mean?
Your founding rate stays the same for 12 months from your first paid invoice. Even when we raise the standard rate, you keep your founding rate. After month 12 your rate auto-migrates to the then-current standard rate unless you renew into a new lock.
Is there a setup fee?
No. Founding clients pay zero setup. Onboarding call, first campaign, dashboard, and credential setup all included.
How does billing work?
Billed monthly, in advance, in CAD plus GST and HST based on your province. Card charged on day 1 of each paid month. Failed payment triggers a 7-day grace window before service pauses.
Can I pause during a slow season?
Yes. One free 30-day or 60-day pause per 12-month cycle. Your rate and channel configuration freeze during the pause.
What if I cancel mid-month?
Service continues through the current billing period. No pro-rata refund. Your data (leads, outreach history, campaigns) is exported to CSV and JSON within 14 days of cancellation.
Who owns the leads?
You do. Every lead Crystallux surfaces is yours to keep even after cancellation.

Only 20 founding slots per vertical.

Founding rate locks for 12 months. When the window closes, the standard rate becomes the new minimum.
