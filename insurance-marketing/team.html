Compliance team

The people who keep us honest

Compliance Officer

Oversees suitability-assessment review, recommendation audit, AML / FINTRAC reporting, FSRA/provincial regulator filings. Reports directly to the Principal.

Privacy Officer

PIPEDA + Quebec Law 25 + Alberta PIPA + BC PIPA. Manages data-handling controls, retention schedules, access requests, and breach response. privacy@crystallux.org

Complaints Officer

First point of contact for complaints. Acknowledges within 2 business days, substantive response within 5. Escalation path to OLHI / GIO / provincial regulator. complaints@crystallux.org
