Why us

Six reasons clients pick Crystallux.

We're not the only MGA in Canada. Here's why people choose us anyway.

AI

AI-powered fit, not just price

Most comparison tools rank by premium. Premium is the wrong question. The right question is: which policy covers the risks you actually face, in your underwriting class, at this carrier's claim experience? Our AI matches on fit; price is one input among many.

📖

Educational, not pushy

We explain trade-offs. We tell you when the cheaper option is actually better for your situation. We tell you when you don't need the cover the last broker tried to sell you. Education is our default; the close happens when you decide, not when we push.

×

Multi-carrier independence

We're not captive. We represent multiple Canadian carriers in life, P&C, and specialty lines. If a competitor's product fits better, we say so. If a Layer 1 carrier won't quote your risk, we know which Layer 2 wholesaler will.

✓

Ethical disclosure

We disclose commission on every recommendation, before you apply. We disclose carrier relationships. We disclose when our compensation is materially different across the options we're showing. That's our ethical floor — beyond the regulatory minimum.

L

LLQP-licensed advisors, always

You're never talking to a non-licensed sales rep, a chatbot, or a call-centre script. Every recommendation, conversation, and application is handled by a licensed insurance professional bound by FSRA / provincial regulator conduct rules.

⚖

Compliance-first culture

Suitability assessment on every recommendation. Audit log on every disclosure. PIPEDA-compliant data handling. AML / FINTRAC reporting where required. Compliance isn't an afterthought — it's how we build trust and how we scale.
