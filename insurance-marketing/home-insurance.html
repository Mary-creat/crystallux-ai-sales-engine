Three property types

Home, condo, or tenant?

Homeowner

Detached, semi, or town. Covers dwelling (the structure), detached structures (shed, garage), contents, additional living expenses, and personal liability. Replacement cost endorsement strongly recommended.

Condo owner

Cover for unit improvements, contents, loss assessments (condo corp claims), and personal liability. Building structure is covered by the condo corporation's master policy.

Tenant

Cover for contents and personal liability inside your rental. Your landlord's policy doesn't protect you. Often required by lease. Typically $200–$400/year.

Coverage essentials

What a standard policy covers

Dwelling

The structure. Set the limit at full replacement cost — not market value. A $1.2M Toronto home might cost $800K to rebuild; that's the dwelling limit.

Contents

Personal belongings. Default is typically 50–70% of dwelling limit. High-value items (jewellery, art, instruments, bikes) need scheduled endorsements above per-category limits.

Personal liability

Pays if you're sued for bodily injury or property damage you caused. Recommended $2M minimum — the premium difference from $1M is trivial.

Endorsements that matter

The gaps in a standard policy

Water damage (overland flooding, surface water)

NOT covered by default — sold as a separate endorsement. Critical in basement-prone areas. Climate change has made this a routine claim — buy it.

Sewer backup

NOT covered by default — sold as a separate endorsement. Cheap ($60–$200/year) relative to the claim ($25K–$80K typical basement-flood claim).

Earthquake

Excluded by default. Recommended in BC (Vancouver, Victoria) and Quebec (Charlevoix region). Often a high deductible (10% of dwelling).

Scheduled jewellery / instruments / fine art

Default contents limit per category is typically $4,000–$10,000. Engagement rings, instruments, art collections need scheduling — appraisal required.

Home business

Default policies exclude business-related claims. A home-business endorsement covers business equipment, business liability, and the home office. Required if you see clients at home.

Service line / equipment breakdown

Cover for buried pipes (water, sewer, gas, electrical) and major appliance/HVAC failure. Newer endorsement; affordable and worth it for older homes.
