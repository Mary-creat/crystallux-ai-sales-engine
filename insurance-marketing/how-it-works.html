How it works

Four steps. Zero pressure.

Most insurance shopping wastes hours on quote forms and pushy phone calls. We compress it to about 30 minutes of your time, spread over a few days, with one human conversation when you want one.

1

Tell us your situation

A 3-minute needs assessment captures the basics, age, family, income, what you're worried about. No medical, no credit pull, no commitment. You can also book a discovery call instead if you prefer to start with a conversation.

Time: 3 minutes online, or 20-minute call.

2

See personalised options

An LLQP-licensed advisor reviews your answers and our matching engine surfaces 2–3 fitted options across our carrier network. We email these to you, with our commission disclosed up front.

Time: 1 business day for our review and email.

3

Compare side-by-side

The email lays it out clearly: coverage, exclusions, riders, premium, claims experience, financial strength. You compare on your time, no pressure. Questions? Reply, or book a call.

Time: 20 minutes when you're ready.

4

Apply with advisor help

When you decide, your advisor walks you through application + underwriting. For life/CI/DI that may include a paramedical exam (we arrange, done at home, free). Policy issues within 2–6 weeks depending on carrier.

Time: 30–60 minutes to apply; underwriting handled by us + the carrier.

Customer experience promise

What we won't do

No cold calls

We never call without an appointment. You book the conversation when you want one, and only when you want one.

No fees to you

Our advice, comparison, application support, and policy management are free. We're paid by the carrier when a policy is placed, and we disclose that commission.

No "best" hype

We don't claim to be the cheapest or the best. We claim to surface options that fit your situation honestly, with the trade-offs explained.

How-it-works FAQ

We're independent, not captive to any single carrier, and we use modern matching tools to speed the search while a licensed advisor reviews every recommendation.
Comparison sites typically sell your lead to whichever broker bids highest. We are the broker, every interaction is with our LLQP-licensed team. We disclose commissions, explain trade-offs, and support you through claims years later. Comparison sites can't do that.
