What tenant insurance does

The three things that get you paid out.

Contents

Your stuff: clothes, electronics, furniture. Replaced if stolen, fire, water damage. Typical $30K-$60K coverage is enough for most renters.

Liability

If you accidentally cause damage to the unit OR injure a visitor. $1M-$2M standard. Lawyers + settlements get expensive fast without it.

Additional living expenses

If your unit becomes unlivable (fire, flood), the insurer pays hotel + meals while you find a new place. Often the most under-appreciated benefit.
