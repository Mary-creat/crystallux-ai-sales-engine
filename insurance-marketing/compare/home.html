What's typically covered

Standard home cover, explained.

Dwelling

The physical structure of your home — walls, roof, foundation. Replacement cost up to your insured value.

Contents

Your personal belongings inside the home. Typically 50-70% of dwelling coverage. Scheduled valuables (jewelry, art) optional add-on.

Liability

If someone is injured on your property OR you accidentally damage someone else's property. Standard $1M, upgrade to $2M is usually $40-80/year.
