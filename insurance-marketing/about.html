About Crystallux Financial Services

Your complete financial protection partner.

Crystallux Financial Services is the financial-services division of Crystallux Inc., a Canadian technology company. We combine modern analysis with LLQP-certified advisor expertise to give Canadians clearer choices and honest disclosure on the products that shape their financial lives.

Section 1

Crystallux Financial Services

Our mission

To give every Canadian access to financial-protection advice that's independent of the carrier, honest about commission, and matched to their actual situation, not the situation the broker would prefer they were in.

What we offer today

Insurance. Seven product lines: life, critical illness, disability, travel, auto, home, business. Multi-carrier independent placement with LLQP-certified advisors across Ontario, Alberta, and BC; provincial expansion through 2026 to Quebec, Manitoba, and the Maritimes.

What's coming next

  • Mortgages, Canadian mortgage brokerage with the same advisor-led + carrier-comparison model. Launching mid-2026.
  • Investments, registered investment dealer offering, including TFSA / RRSP / non-registered portfolios. Launching Q4 2026.
  • Retirement planning, integrated retirement-income modelling for clients with combined Crystallux investment + insurance plans. 2027.
  • Estate planning, coordinated with our insurance and investment lines; will-and-trust referral network. 2027.

Why we exist

Most Canadians get financial advice from one of three places: a captive agent (selling one carrier's products), a comparison aggregator (selling the lead to whichever broker bids highest), or a bank (selling whatever the bank manufactures). All three have structural conflicts. We built a fourth option, an institutionally-disclosed, multi-carrier brokerage where we do the carrier comparison and a licensed advisor reviews every recommendation before it reaches the client.

Our approach

Three commitments: (1) we disclose commission on every recommendation before you apply; (2) we present 2–3 carrier options with the trade-offs explained, not the carrier with the highest commission; (3) we tell you when none of our products is right for you, including referring you to a competitor when that serves you better.

Our compliance posture

LLQP certification for every advisor on the life / accident / sickness side; P&C licensing where required. $2M E&O insurance. PIPEDA, Quebec Law 25, Alberta PIPA, BC PIPA compliant. AML / FINTRAC registered. CASL-compliant on all marketing.

"I started Crystallux because I watched too many people get the wrong policy from the wrong broker for the wrong reason. The technology to fix that exists. The ethics to use it well, that's the harder part."

Background

Mary holds an LLQP certification (Life License Qualification Program), the credential required to give life, accident, and sickness insurance advice in Canada. She is the Founder & Principal of Crystallux Inc., the parent company of Crystallux Financial Services.

Why she founded Crystallux

Mary's path to Crystallux started with personal experience: family members getting sold over-priced policies from captive agents, and friends getting their data farmed by comparison aggregators that didn't actually compare anything. The Crystallux thesis: a single platform combining honest carrier comparison, ethical disclosure, and LLQP-certified human advice produces better outcomes than any one of those alone.

Vision for Crystallux Financial Services

Insurance is the first product line because it's where the disclosure and matching problem is most acute. Mortgages, investments, retirement planning and estate planning all share the same structural conflict, and the same solution. The 2026–2027 roadmap brings each of those lines under the Crystallux Financial Services brand, with the same disclosure-first ethic.
