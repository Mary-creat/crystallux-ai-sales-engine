About

Insurance advice without the salesy bit.

Crystallux Insurance Network is a Canadian insurance MGA based in Guelph, Ontario. We built it because we wanted what we couldn't find: independent multi-carrier insurance advice with the underwriting trade-offs explained, the commissions disclosed, and the AI doing the matching grunt-work so humans can focus on the conversation.

Founder

Mary Akintunde

Mary Akintunde founded Crystallux Insurance Network in 2026. LLQP-licensed (license # to be added). FSRA-registered. Based in Guelph, Ontario, serving clients Canada-wide.

She started Crystallux after watching family members get sold the wrong products by captive agents and seeing comparison sites farm their personal data to whichever broker bid highest. The Crystallux thesis: an MGA built on AI matching + ethical disclosure + LLQP advice can produce better outcomes than either captive sales or commodity comparison.

Mary also operates Victory Enrichment, a registered Canadian non-profit. Any commercial relationship between Crystallux Insurance Network and Victory Enrichment is governed by an arms-length partnership framework reviewed by non-profit counsel. Mission

What we're building

Honest matching

AI does the grunt work — cross-carrier coverage matching, exclusion comparison, rate estimation. Humans do the judgment — what trade-offs make sense for your specific life. The combination produces better recommendations than either alone.

Disclosure-first

We disclose commissions up front. We disclose carrier relationships. We disclose when our compensation varies materially across the options shown. Most brokers hide this. We lead with it.

Educational always

Insurance is one of the largest financial decisions most families make. We treat clients like adults — explaining trade-offs, naming gaps, refusing to oversell. Education compounds; trust compounds.

Compliance commitment

How we operate

  • FSRA-regulated — Ontario Financial Services Regulatory Authority oversees our principal regulator. Licensing extends to other provincial regulators as we serve clients in those provinces.
  • LLQP-licensed advisors — Life License Qualification Program. Required for life, accident, and sickness insurance in every province except Quebec (where AMF licensing applies).
  • E&O insured — $2,000,000 minimum errors-and-omissions coverage protecting clients against advisor mistakes.
  • PIPEDA-compliant — Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act. Plus Quebec Law 25 for Quebec residents. Plus Alberta PIPA / BC PIPA equivalents.
  • AML / FINTRAC — Anti-Money-Laundering reporting program. PCMLTFA registration where applicable.
  • CASL-compliant — Canada Anti-Spam Legislation. Express consent on every contact channel. No purchased lead lists.
  • Suitability assessment on every recommendation — documented audit trail per FSRA / provincial regulator standards.
