Mary's own outbound machine: prospecting Crystallux to Canadian brokers, contractors, agents, consultants, and dentists. This is Crystallux-selling-Crystallux — meta, and critical. The product becomes its own first customer.

## Suggested deployment sequence

### Pre-launch (days 1-5 post-contract-sign)

1. **MARY_TARGET_LIST_BUILDER.md** — set up Sales Navigator + Apollo accounts; build first 100-person target list; configure HubSpot pipeline stages
2. **DAILY_OUTREACH_CADENCE.md** — establish the 30-minute daily habit; pick a consistent time

### Launch day (1 day, ~day 5-10)

3. **LAUNCH_ANNOUNCEMENT.md** — coordinated launch: LinkedIn posts + email to network + 10-person amplification. Pick Tuesday or Wednesday, mid-month.

### Month 1 (daily, every business day)

4. Execute the daily cadence. 100 touches/week. 20-30 discovery calls booked over the month.
5. Convert discovery calls → POC or founding contracts per the first-call scripts in `docs/client-outreach/`

### Month 2+ (proactive retention)

6. **REFERRAL_PROGRAM.md** — introduce to each client during month-2 weekly check-in (after first-30-day guarantee has been met). First referrals start compounding pipeline.

## Principles applied across this folder

- **Compounding over heroic.** 30 minutes every business day beats 3 hours once a week.
- **Personal > automated.** Mary's own manual touches for the first 3 months; automate only when volume justifies.
- **Quality over quantity.** 100 perfectly-targeted touches > 500 broadly-targeted ones.
- **Tracked, not vibes.** Every touch in HubSpot with pipeline stage. Untracked outreach is invisible.
- **Referrals are the cheapest channel.** Nothing outranks client-sourced referrals; the referral program is structured to accelerate that.
