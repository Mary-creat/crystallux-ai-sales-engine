Features

Every capability Crystallux ships. No upsell gates.

Founding clients get the full platform. The only thing that changes at higher tiers is send volume, add-on channels, and strategic support.

Prospect sourcing

A

Apollo enrichment

Every lead gets firmographic enrichment — company size, industry, org chart, decision-maker titles. Filtered to your exact ICP.

L

LinkedIn sourcing

Sales Navigator-quality targeting via Unipile. Decision-maker filters cut noise by 80%; the names you get are worth the outreach.

✦

Intent signals

Per-vertical signals surface leads 30–60 days before they start shopping: renewal-window timing, reno-planning signals, pre-MLS listings, treatment-plan gaps.

Personalised outreach

C

Claude-authored copy

Every email, LinkedIn message, SMS, and voice script is written by Claude for the specific lead. No templates. No mail-merge. Reads like a peer wrote it.

◨

Niche overlays

Seven vertical overlays govern tone, pain-signal taxonomy, compliance constraints, and channel mix. One universal engine, seven distinct voices.

✓

CASL compliance

Every outreach carries sender identification, business address, and unsubscribe. Compliance templates reviewed per province for regulated verticals (dental, legal).

Multi-channel delivery

✉

Email

Gmail-delivered, warmed-up, deliverability-monitored. First channel on every plan.

in

LinkedIn

Unipile-powered connection requests + first-message sequences. Compliant with LinkedIn's automation policies.

sms

SMS / WhatsApp

Twilio-delivered with strict CASL consent checks. Critical for residential verticals.

📞

Voice

Vapi-powered AI voice calls with DNCL compliance gate. Canadian-specific — real CRTC check required before activation.

▶

Video

Tavus-generated personalised videos with your replica. 60-second peer-to-peer scripts tuned per lead.

↵

Unified reply routing

Every reply — regardless of channel — routes to your calendar + notification inbox within minutes.

Dashboard + operations

◉

Real-time dashboard

Pipeline stats, lead grid, outreach log, billing, channels active. Auto-refreshes every 60 seconds. Mobile-responsive.

$

Stripe billing

Monthly in CAD, plus GST/HST. Customer portal for card updates and invoice history. No setup fee for founding clients.

△

Error monitoring

Every workflow failure, rate limit breach, and deliverability flag surfaces on the dashboard with severity-based thresholds.

🔒

Client isolation

Row-level security hardened. Every client sees their own data only. Separate dashboard URL per client.

✦

Admin Copilot

For operators: conversational AI for database queries, error troubleshooting, and platform Q&A. Admin-only.

Calendly booking

Qualified replies route directly to your Calendly. You see booked meetings; no chasing.
