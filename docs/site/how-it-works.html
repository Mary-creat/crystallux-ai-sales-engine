How it works

From contract to full pipeline in 21 days.

Here's exactly what happens after you sign. No mystery. No "it depends." A clear timeline with a guarantee at the end.

Day 0 · Contract + onboarding call

Sign the agreement. 30-minute onboarding call that same day or the next. We capture your ICP (top 3 customer types, size, service area, vertical), channel preferences (email always on; LinkedIn, SMS, voice, video opt-in), Calendly URL, sender-email config, and any existing leads you want preserved.

What you bring

Your Calendly link, access to a business email for outreach delivery, and a 1-sentence description of your top 3 customer types.

Day 1–3 · Prospect sourcing

We load the first 50 prospects matching your ICP from Apollo + LinkedIn + vertical-specific intent signals. Lead-research runs overnight: Claude drafts a one-paragraph summary + the most-relevant pain signal for each prospect.

What you see

Your dashboard URL is live. 50 researched leads waiting. Review them, flag any off-target ones, and we refine the filter.

Day 4–7 · First outreach

Claude generates the first batch of personalised outreach — emails (always), plus any channels you've activated. You review the first 10–15 drafts. Flag anything that feels off; we tune the prompt. Once approved, the system sends.

Peer-reviewed copy

A founding insurance broker rated our cold outreach 9.5/10 — said it was the first one that sounded like a peer, not a vendor. That's the bar.

Day 7–14 · Replies + follow-ups

Qualified replies flow into your Calendly + notification email. The system runs day-3 and day-7 follow-ups automatically on non-responders. You only step in at the conversation stage — no time on copy, cadence, or follow-up timing.

Day 14 · First checkpoint call

15-minute call. Wins, metrics review, blockers, one optimisation for the next week. Weekly cadence continues through month 3, then scales to bi-weekly.

Day 21 · Full pipeline running

By the end of week 3, you have a consistent daily outreach flow, qualified meetings booking on your calendar, and a reliable week-over-week cadence. The guarantee window runs through day 30 — if we haven't hit your vertical's target by then, your next month is free.

Universal engine, vertical-specific depth

The Crystallux engine is one platform. Every vertical has its own overlay: pain-signal taxonomy, outreach tone, compliance constraints (RIBO for insurance, RCDSO for dental, LSO for legal), preferred channel mix, and pricing. You're not getting a generic SaaS tool tuned with a config flag — you're getting copy that reads like a peer in your trade wrote it.
