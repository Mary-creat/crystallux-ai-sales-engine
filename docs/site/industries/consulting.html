Pain signals we target

Solo strategy consultants, boutique firms (2–10 partners), fractional execs. One-person marketing department. The pain of being the rainmaker and the delivery lead.

  • Feast-or-famine revenue with 30–60 day dry spells
  • Weekends writing proposals instead of delivering
  • Dependence on LinkedIn + past-client referrals
  • Losing RFPs to bigger firms with marketing budgets
  • Utilisation below 60% between engagements
  • Discovery calls that don't convert due to weak pre-qualification
  • Can't charge premium rates without case studies
  • Hiring a BDR is too expensive at current revenue stage

Every outreach is Claude-authored as a fellow consultant writing to a peer — not a vendor pitching a buyer. No jargon. No "Hi [FIRST_NAME]." No bulletpointed value props. Short, confident sentences that acknowledge the realities of solo practice and boutique-firm economics.
