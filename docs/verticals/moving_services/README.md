# Moving & Relocation Services — Vertical Profile

**Rank:** #6 (INACTIVE)
**Founding price:** $997/mo CAD · **Retail:** $1,497/mo · **Upsell:** $2,497/mo (Growth Pro)
**Target outcome:** 30 qualified move leads/month
**Guarantee:** 15 quotes booked in first 30 days or month free.

## Why this vertical is INACTIVE

Unit economics are tighter. $997 founding price is the lowest in the catalog, margin on platform fee is thinner, and moving businesses have higher operational churn (seasonal revenue swings, cancellation risk). Activate this vertical only once the top-4 active verticals have ≥3 paying clients each and Crystallux has proven operating leverage. The playbook works; the market just requires more operational maturity before scaling.

## Three personas (for when activated)

1. **Owner-operator (1-3 trucks)** — owner drives one truck, dispatches the others, hands-on scheduling. Price-sensitive. Summer boom, winter slump.
2. **Growing regional mover (4-10 trucks)** — starting to systemise, has a dispatcher, looking to stabilise winter revenue.
3. **Commercial relocation specialist** — office + equipment moves, higher AOV, longer cycles, B2B pipeline.

## Pain signals (from migration)

1. Losing jobs to last-minute cancellations
2. Competing with Two Men and a Truck on brand
3. U-Haul and PODS stealing DIY-price-sensitive customers
4. Moving aggregators capping rates
5. Seasonal income swings, summer boom, winter famine
6. Crew turnover during peak moving season
7. Customer ghosting after quote
8. No-show customers costing a truck plus crew half-day
9. Fuel prices eating into margins
10. Insurance premiums climbing each year
11. Review sites damaging reputation
12. Cannot predict lead flow month to month

## Offer angle (peer-operator)

"You hit a wall in winter and you're fighting last-minute cancellations all summer. We find homeowners planning moves 2-3 weeks before they start calling for quotes. Lock them in before Two Men shows up. 30 quality quotes a month. 15 booked in the first 30 days or the month is free."
