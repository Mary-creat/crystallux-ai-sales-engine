**Rank:** #1 (active)

## Why this vertical ranks #1

Consultants buy the fastest. They understand the unit economics of a discovery call instantly, they have revenue to spend on growth, and they're the audience most likely to recognise what Crystallux does without a full demo. Their pain (feast-or-famine pipeline, bizdev eating weekends) maps 1:1 to the product. Close cycle: 3-10 days from first touch to signed contract when the case landed.

## Three personas

1. **Solo strategy consultant** — ex-McKinsey/BCG/Bain, 3-15 years solo, billings $250-600K/yr. Spends weekends writing proposals, wants pipeline system. Price-insensitive on $2K/mo if it books one $50K engagement.
2. **Boutique firm founder (2-10 consultants)** — hit $1-3M revenue, can't hire a full-time BDR, utilisation dropping below 65%, partners each bringing their own bizdev pipeline inconsistently. Needs a shared lead engine.
3. **Fractional exec (CFO, CTO, CMO)** — 2-4 concurrent engagements, wants to fill the 5th slot but doesn't have a marketing function. Highly technical buyer, will ask for the integration stack.

## Pain signals (from migration)

1. Feast-or-famine revenue with 30-60 day dry spells
2. Spending evenings writing proposals instead of delivering
3. Dependence on LinkedIn outreach and past-client referrals
4. Losing RFPs to bigger firms with larger marketing budgets
5. Utilisation dropping below 60 percent between engagements
6. Discovery calls that don't convert because pre-qualification is weak
7. Can't charge premium rates without case studies
8. Bizdev eating weekends that should be rest or family time
9. Website hasn't been updated in 18 months, stale positioning
10. Cold outreach feels beneath the brand but warm pipeline is empty
11. Conference ROI unclear, business cards go nowhere
12. Hiring a BDR is too expensive for the revenue stage

## Offer angle (peer-to-peer)

"I stop trading billable hours for bizdev hours. You keep delivering; we fill the pipeline. 20 qualified discovery calls per month with decision-makers, not gatekeepers. If we don't book 10 in your first 30 days, the month is free."
