**Rank:** #7 (INACTIVE)

**Target outcome:** 25 qualified cleaning leads/month prioritizing recurring contracts
**Guarantee:** 10 booked cleans in first 30 days or month free.

## Why this vertical is INACTIVE

Same structural issue as moving: $997 AOV with high-churn client-side customers makes platform unit economics difficult at small scale. Cleaning businesses also depend heavily on cleaner retention — if the operator can't deliver consistent service, Crystallux delivering leads doesn't help. Activate after core operating leverage is in place and at least one cleaning-vertical client signs as a founding reference.

## Three personas (for when activated)

1. **Owner-operator (1-5 cleaners)** — owner supervises every job. Growth capped by owner's attention. Needs a system that books recurring contracts so owner can step back.
2. **Growing residential cleaning company (6-20 cleaners)** — has a supervisor role, tracking quality is the bottleneck. Churn from Merry Maids / Molly Maid brand awareness.
3. **Commercial janitorial specialist** — office buildings, retail chains. B2B audience, longer cycles, higher LTV per account.

## Pain signals (from migration)

1. Customers booking one-time cleans and ghosting
2. Losing regulars to Merry Maids or Molly Maid marketing
3. Cleaner turnover training cost eating profit
4. Spending on Google Ads for one-time bookings
5. Last-minute cancellations wasting scheduled hours
6. Trying to build recurring contracts but competitors undercut
7. Cannot scale beyond what I can personally supervise
8. Commercial contracts stuck in bid wars on price
9. Customers comparing to 15-per-hour individual cleaners
10. Review management across platforms consumes time
11. Seasonal spikes, spring cleaning rush then quiet
12. No retention system, customers drift away silently

## Offer angle (peer-operator)

"You're stuck scaling past what you can supervise personally. Merry Maids is eating your regulars with a marketing budget you can't match. We find homeowners wanting weekly or bi-weekly recurring cleaning — higher LTV than one-time jobs. 25 qualified recurring leads a month. 10 booked in the first 30 days or the month is free."
