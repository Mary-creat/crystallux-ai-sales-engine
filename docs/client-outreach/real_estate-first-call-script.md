# Real Estate — First Discovery Call Script

**Duration:** 15 minutes. **Goal:** signed contract same-call or within 24 hours. Agents decide fast.

---

## 1. Opening (45 seconds)

> "Hey [Name], Mary from Crystallux. Quick one. Before I pitch anything, I want to understand where you are in your year — how many deals so far, what your farm area is, and where the listing pipeline is coming from right now."

---

## 2. Pain diagnosis (5 questions, ~5 minutes)

1. **Volume:** "How many deals year-to-date, and what's your 12-month target?"
2. **Farm area:** "What's your primary farm area? City, neighborhood, or both?"
3. **Pipeline source:** "Right now, where do your listings actually come from — sphere of influence, Zillow-type aggregators, FSBO lists, cold door-knocks?"
4. **Cost per listing:** "What's it actually costing you per listing right now, all-in? Ad spend, aggregator fees, hours of your own time?"
5. **What would change the year:** "If you added 5 more listings per month starting now, what would that do for you?"

---

## 3. Offer presentation (3 minutes)

> "Here's how Crystallux works for agents. We find homeowners planning to list 30-60 days before they hit the MLS. We surface them to you with the detail you need to book a listing appointment — not just a name and number. You get in front of them before any other agent knows they exist.
>
> Founding price is $1,497/month CAD, locked for 12 months. Target is 15 listing appointments a month in your farm area. Your guarantee: 5 listing appointments in your first 30 days, or your next month is free. One commission pays this back 12 months over."

---

## 4. Common objection + answer

**"I already pay $600/mo for Zillow Premier Agent — why would I pay more?"**

> "Zillow sells the same lead to 5 agents in your zip code. You're paying for the fight, not the listing. We find sellers who aren't on Zillow yet — no competition. If you want to keep Premier for buyer leads, fine. Crystallux is for the seller side, which is 70% of your commission anyway."

**"My market is slow right now."**

> "Slow markets are when this matters most. The agents who grow in a down market are the ones talking to sellers before their neighbor does. Every seller who lists is one who didn't pick you. This is the lever."

---

## 5. Close (2 minutes)

> "Fastest version: I send the contract now, we kick off onboarding tomorrow, your first listing appointments are booked by end of week 2. Are you ready, or do you want to talk to your broker first?"

Agents often check with broker-of-record on marketing spend. Standard move: send a short email they can forward to their broker that emphasises the ROI math on one listing.

---

## Conversion targets

- **Signed same-call:** ≥40% (agents decide fast)
- **Signed within 24h:** ≥60% cumulative
- **Average sale price:** $1,497 founding
- **Upsell to Growth Pro ($2,997):** ≥25% within 90 days (teams + multi-agent offices)
