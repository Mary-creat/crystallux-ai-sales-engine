# Consulting — First Discovery Call Script

**Duration:** 20 minutes. **Goal:** signed contract or a clear next step within 48 hours.

---

## 1. Opening (60 seconds)

> "Hey [Name], thanks for taking the call. I'm Mary from Crystallux. Before I talk about what we do, I'd rather just understand your practice first — what you do, who you do it for, and where the pipeline pain is right now. Sound good?"

---

## 2. Pain diagnosis (5 questions, ~8 minutes)

1. **Engagement mix:** "What does a typical month look like for you — how many active engagements, how many paused, how much utilisation?"
2. **Pipeline source:** "Where's your current pipeline coming from — referrals, LinkedIn, your network? What percentage of each?"
3. **Dry spells:** "What's the longest dry spell you've had in the last 12 months? What happened coming out of it?"
4. **Bizdev time:** "How many hours a week are you personally spending on sales and bizdev? What would you do with that time if it came back?"
5. **The ideal:** "If you could wake up tomorrow with one thing fixed in your business, what would it be?"

---

## 3. Offer presentation (4 minutes)

> "Here's what Crystallux does, tuned to consultants like you. We run your top-of-funnel — the cold outreach, the qualification, the pre-meeting screening. You keep delivering the work. Every week you get a calendar full of discovery calls with decision-makers we've qualified, not gatekeepers and not tire-kickers. Target is 20 qualified discovery calls a month.
>
> Founding price locked for 12 months is $1,997/month CAD. That's it. No setup fee, no per-lead charge, no long-term contract beyond the first three months. Your guarantee: 10 qualified meetings in your first 30 days, or your second month is free."

---

## 4. Common objection + answer

**"How is this different from an SDR or an agency?"**

> "An SDR costs you $8-12K/month fully loaded and requires you to manage them. An agency costs $3-5K/month and you still write the sequences. We run the system — the outreach templates are Claude-authored against your niche, the qualification logic is in the platform, and the meeting booking is fully automated. You manage the output, not the process. Closest comparison is an in-house BDR at a fraction of the cost."

**"What happens if I only get 5 meetings in 30 days?"**

> "Month free. Not a partial credit, not a future discount — your next month's $1,997 doesn't come off your card. We eat the risk because we're confident in the system. After that, we keep iterating until you hit 10."

---

## 5. Close (2-3 minutes)

> "Two paths from here. Path one: you're in. I send a contract today, we kick off onboarding tomorrow, you see your first discovery call booked in week two.
>
> Path two: you need to think. Totally fair. I'll send you a one-page summary plus one case study. What's the earliest we can reconnect — Thursday next week?"

Capture: preferred plan, exact company name for invoicing, whether they want a 14-day trial or go straight to paid (most consultants go straight; they price things in billable-hour math instantly).

---

## Conversion targets (track per call)

- **Signed same-call:** ≥30%
- **Signed within 48h (post-follow-up):** ≥55% cumulative
- **Lost / no-decision after 7 days:** ≤20%
- **Average sale price:** $1,997 founding (aim to upsell 20% into $3,997 Growth Pro in month 2)
