# Cleaning Services — First Discovery Call Script

**Status:** draft — vertical INACTIVE (see `docs/verticals/cleaning_services/README.md`).
**Duration:** 15 minutes. **Goal:** signed contract same-call or within 48h.
**Timing:** cleaning owners typically available 9-11am after morning dispatch, or 4-6pm.

---

## 1. Opening (30 seconds)

> "Hey [Name], Mary from Crystallux. Quick one — I work with cleaning and janitorial businesses across Canada. Before I pitch anything, I want to understand what's actually hurting right now: one-time-clean ghosting, losing regulars to Merry Maids, or cleaner turnover eating your margin?"

---

## 2. Pain diagnosis (5 questions, ~5 minutes)

1. **Client mix:** "What's the split between one-time cleans and recurring contracts?"
2. **Crew size:** "How many cleaners, how many jobs per week?"
3. **Customer source:** "Where do customers come from — Google, Facebook, referrals, or are you still cold-door-knocking?"
4. **Retention:** "What's your 6-month retention on recurring contracts?"
5. **Scale ceiling:** "Where does the business plateau right now — is it leads, cleaner supply, or supervision capacity?"

---

## 3. Offer presentation (3 minutes)

> "Crystallux for cleaning businesses. We find homeowners wanting weekly or bi-weekly recurring cleaning — high-LTV customers, not one-time cleaners shopping on price. You book recurring contracts. LTV goes up. Churn goes down.
>
> Founding price $997/month CAD, locked for 12 months. Target: 25 qualified recurring-contract leads a month. Guarantee: 10 booked cleans in your first 30 days or the month is free."

---

## 4. Common objection + answer

**"I already have more cleans than I can staff."**

> "Cleaner supply is a different problem than lead supply — we don't solve cleaner turnover. But if your issue is scaling past your supervision capacity, we can pace the lead flow. You set the monthly cap; we don't flood you."

**"I get lots of one-time cleans; why do I need recurring?"**

> "LTV math. A one-time clean is $200. A bi-weekly contract is $200 × 26 = $5,200/year. Plus referrals. Same lead volume, 20x the revenue."

---

## 5. Close (2 minutes)

> "Sign today, onboard tomorrow, first recurring-contract leads in 4-5 days. Or book a follow-up for Thursday. Which?"

---

## Conversion targets

- **Signed within 48h:** ≥30%
- **Signed within 7 days:** ≥50% cumulative
- **Average sale price:** $997 founding
- **Upsell to Retention Pro ($1,997):** ≥25% after month 3
