# Moving Services — First Discovery Call Script

**Status:** draft — vertical INACTIVE until top-4 actives mature (see `docs/verticals/moving_services/README.md`).
**Duration:** 15 minutes. **Goal:** contract signed same-call or within 48h.
**Timing:** call early-morning (7-8am) or late-afternoon (4-6pm). Avoid peak dispatch hours.

---

## 1. Opening (30 seconds)

> "Hey [Name], Mary from Crystallux. Quick one — I work with moving companies across Canada on lead flow. Before I pitch, where is your pipeline at right now, and what's killing you most: winter slump, cancellations, or U-Haul DIY pressure?"

---

## 2. Pain diagnosis (5 questions, ~5 minutes)

1. **Fleet size:** "How many trucks, how many crews, how many moves last month?"
2. **Lead sources:** "Where do leads come from — Google, aggregators like MovingWaldo, referrals, repeat customers?"
3. **Quote-to-book:** "What percentage of quotes convert to booked moves? And of booked moves, what's your no-show rate?"
4. **Seasonal swings:** "What's your revenue split between peak (May-Sep) and off-season (Oct-April)?"
5. **If winter was busy:** "If you booked 30 moves in January, what would that change for you?"

---

## 3. Offer presentation (3 minutes)

> "Crystallux for moving companies. We find homeowners 2-3 weeks before they start shopping quotes. You get to them first — before Two Men and a Truck shows up on their Google results, before aggregators rate-shop them. Residential focus with commercial relocation as an add-on.
>
> Founding price $997/month CAD, locked for 12 months. Target: 30 qualified move leads per month. Guarantee: 15 quotes booked in your first 30 days or the month is free."

---

## 4. Common objection + answer

**"I tried lead aggregators, got burned."**

> "Aggregators sell the same lead to 5 movers. You pay for the fight. We source leads that aren't on aggregators — homeowners we find through intent signals and home-sale data. You're the first call, not the fifth."

**"I can't afford $997/mo in winter."**

> "One booked move in winter pays for the month. Target is 15 booked moves in your first 30 days. If we miss, month's free. If we hit, you'll wonder why you waited."

---

## 5. Close (2 minutes)

> "Two paths. Sign today, onboard tomorrow, first leads hit by day 5. Or we book a follow-up for 48 hours. Which one?"

---

## Conversion targets

- **Signed within 48h:** ≥35%
- **Signed within 7 days:** ≥55% cumulative
- **Average sale price:** $997 founding
- **Upsell to Growth Pro ($2,497):** ≥20% after month 3 (multi-truck operators)
