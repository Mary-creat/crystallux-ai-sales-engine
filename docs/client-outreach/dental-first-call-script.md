# Dental — First Discovery Call Script

**Duration:** 20 minutes (dentists prefer structured calls). **Goal:** contract signed within 10 business days after compliance copy review.
**Timing:** book for lunch hours (12-1pm) or after 5pm. Never during clinical blocks.

---

## 1. Opening (60 seconds)

> "Hi Dr. [Name], Mary from Crystallux. I work with practice owners across [Ontario/BC/Alberta] on patient acquisition. Before I walk you through anything, I'd like to just understand your practice — number of operatories, hygiene team, where new-patient flow is coming from, and where the quiet chair hours are."

---

## 2. Pain diagnosis (5 questions, ~8 minutes)

1. **Practice setup:** "How many operatories, how many active hygienists, what's your average new-patient volume per month?"
2. **Chair utilisation:** "Which days of the week and which times see empty chairs right now?"
3. **Acquisition cost:** "What's your per-patient acquisition cost today? Between Google Ads, referral cards, and your existing marketing mix?"
4. **Recall load:** "How many hours per week is your front desk spending on recall calls? Are they hitting target rebooking rates?"
5. **Growth goal:** "If I could book your quiet chair hours with qualified new-patient consults over the next 90 days, what would that mean for practice growth?"

---

## 3. Offer presentation (5 minutes)

> "Here's Crystallux for dental practices. We identify families and individuals in your service area actively looking for a new dentist — people who moved recently, people unhappy with their current practice, people with cosmetic-case signals. We pre-qualify them and book new-patient consults directly into your chair.
>
> Founding price is $1,497/month CAD, locked for 12 months. Target: 30 qualified new-patient consults per month. Guarantee: 15 booked consults in your first 30 days or your second month is free.
>
> Every outreach template is reviewed for your provincial dental regulatory body before it sends — no outcome claims, no diagnostic claims, fully compliant."

---

## 4. Common objection + answer

**"My front desk is maxed out already — more new patients will break them."**

> "We handle the booking. The platform puts confirmed appointments directly into your practice management software [Dentrix / OpenDental / ClearDent]. Your front desk doesn't field the calls; they see the appointments land in the schedule. We can also layer in a treatment-plan follow-up sequence if that's part of your bottleneck."

**"I don't want to run marketing that will get me in trouble with RCDSO / ADA."**

> "Neither do we. Every template you send gets reviewed against [Ontario RCDSO / BC College of Dental Surgeons / Alberta Dental Association] rules before it goes out. We specifically avoid outcome language, comparative language, and diagnostic claims. If a copy revision is ever needed, we do it within 48h. This is a compliance-first product."

---

## 5. Close (3 minutes)

> "Here's the process. Today we decide if this is a fit. If yes, I send a contract this afternoon. Over the next 5 business days we do copy review with your compliance input — you see every template before anything sends. Week 2 we launch, and your first consults are booked by end of that week.
>
> Want to see a sample template stack, or do you want to move forward with the contract?"

Dentists often ask for 48h to check with their practice manager or business partner. Standard reply: "Completely understood. Let me send the contract + compliance template package tonight. Can we book a 30-minute follow-up Wednesday to address any flags?"

---

## Conversion targets

- **Signed within 7 days:** ≥40%
- **Signed within 14 days (post-compliance review):** ≥60% cumulative
- **Average sale price:** $1,497 founding
- **Upsell to Growth Pro ($2,997):** ≥30% within 90 days (multi-location + cosmetic practices)
