# Legal — First Discovery Call Script

**Status:** draft — do not use until vertical is activated (see `docs/verticals/legal/README.md` for blockers).
**Duration:** 30 minutes (lawyers expect longer, measured calls). **Goal:** signed contract within 30 days after compliance copy review.

---

## 1. Opening (60 seconds)

> "Hi [Name], Mary from Crystallux. I work with solo lawyers and boutique firms across Canada on intake pipeline — specifically, building predictable retainer flow while staying inside law-society advertising rules. Before I describe anything, I'd like to understand your practice first."

---

## 2. Pain diagnosis (5 questions, ~10 minutes)

1. **Practice mix:** "What practice areas are you active in right now? What's your typical file mix per month?"
2. **Retainer flow:** "How variable is retainer flow month to month? What's the gap between your best and worst month in the last year?"
3. **Referral dependence:** "Where does your new-client flow actually come from — referring lawyers, past clients, Google, directories?"
4. **Intake staff:** "Do you have dedicated intake, or is reception handling initial calls? What percentage of calls turn into retainers?"
5. **Compliance comfort:** "What's your relationship with your law society's advertising rules? Have you had compliance concerns with past marketing?"

---

## 3. Offer presentation (8 minutes — lawyers evaluate on detail)

> "Here's Crystallux for legal practices. We build an intake pipeline for your practice areas — family, immigration, real estate, corporate, employment, whatever you're authorised to practice. We pre-qualify leads for conflict-check compatibility and matter-type fit, then route them to your intake person or directly to your calendar.
>
> Every outreach template is reviewed against your law society's rules before it sends — no outcome claims, no comparative advertising, no testimonial references without written consent on file.
>
> Founding price is $1,997/month CAD, locked for 12 months. Target: 15 qualified intake consults per month. Guarantee: 8 qualified intakes in your first 30 days or the month is free."

---

## 4. Common objection + answer

**"I'm worried about LSO / Law Society compliance on outreach."**

> "So are we. That's why the first 10-14 days of onboarding is a compliance-review cycle — you or your trusted advising colleague reviews every template before anything sends. We maintain per-province variants so if you're licensed in multiple jurisdictions, the copy adjusts. If LSO updates its advertising rules, we adjust and notify you within 48 hours."

**"My practice is referral-based. This feels beneath the brand."**

> "The residential segment isn't for your brand — it's for the emerging-client base that doesn't know your name yet. The commercial segment — business owners needing corporate counsel — is pure professional outreach that reads like a qualified referral. We can start with just one segment and see how it feels."

---

## 5. Close (4 minutes)

> "Here's the structure. Today we decide if there's a fit. If yes, contract signs and we begin a 10-14 day compliance review cycle — you review every template, you flag anything that concerns you, we adjust. Once you sign off, we launch. First intake consults show in weeks 3-4.
>
> Shall I send you the contract and compliance review pack today, or do you want to consult with a colleague first?"

Lawyers routinely check with managing partner, advising counsel, or their law society advisor before signing. Assume 7-21 day decision cycle.

---

## Conversion targets

- **Signed within 14 days:** ≥25%
- **Signed within 30 days:** ≥50% cumulative
- **Average sale price:** $1,997 founding
- **Upsell to Practice Growth ($3,997):** ≥20% within 180 days (multi-lawyer boutiques)

**When activating this vertical, expect 2x the normal CAC until the first 2 reference clients are in place.**
