# Construction — First Discovery Call Script

**Duration:** 15 minutes. **Goal:** contract signed or clear next step within 72 hours.
**Timing tip:** call early morning (6:30-7:30am) before crews roll, or lunch (noon-1pm). Evening okay.

---

## 1. Opening (45 seconds)

> "Hey [Name], this is Mary from Crystallux. Are you in the middle of something — 10 minutes okay or do you want me to call back?"

(Pause for answer. If busy, book back for 11 seconds later.)

> "Quick context, then I just want to hear where your pipeline is at right now. I work with residential reno contractors and small commercial GCs. No pitch in the first 5 minutes, promise."

---

## 2. Pain diagnosis (5 questions, ~5 minutes)

1. **Current pipeline:** "What does your next 90 days look like — booked, bidding, or flat?"
2. **Lead sources:** "Where are leads coming from? HomeStars, Houzz, Google Ads, referrals, door-knocks, all of the above?"
3. **Cost per lead:** "What are you paying per lead right now? And what percentage of those actually convert to a quote, not just a phone tag?"
4. **Crew utilisation:** "How many weeks of downtime did you have last year between projects?"
5. **If you had 20 quality quotes:** "If I handed you 20 qualified reno quotes next month — homeowners who are 30-60 days from starting, not tire-kickers — what would you do with them?"

---

## 3. Offer presentation (3 minutes)

> "Here's what we do. We find homeowners 30-60 days before they start calling contractors. Kitchens, basements, additions, aging-home repairs. You get them first — before Houzz, before HomeStars, before the three other GCs your neighbor recommended.
>
> Founding price is $1,497/month CAD, locked for 12 months. Target: 20 quality quotes per month in your service area. Guarantee: 10 qualified leads in your first 30 days or the month is free. One signed reno pays this back."

---

## 4. Common objection + answer

**"I don't have time to follow up on more leads — I'm drowning."**

> "We automate the follow-up too. SMS, email, appointment reminder. You only step in when the homeowner wants to meet for a quote. Your job is the estimate and the site visit. Everything before that we handle."

**"My Google Ads guy already says he can do this."**

> "Google Ads shows your banner to people who searched 'contractor near me' last night. Half are shopping, half are tire-kickers. We find homeowners 30-60 days before they search. Different problem, different solution. Test us one month side-by-side and see which leads actually book a quote."

---

## 5. Close (2 minutes)

> "Two options. One: I email the contract now, we start onboarding tomorrow, you see your first leads by day 7. Two: you want to think. Fine — I'll send a one-pager. When can we talk again, Thursday morning?"

Construction owners often say "let me think about it until this weekend." Standard reply: "Fair. I'll check in Sunday evening if we haven't caught up." Keep the follow-up tight.

---

## Conversion targets

- **Signed within 72h:** ≥45%
- **Signed within 7 days:** ≥65% cumulative
- **Average sale price:** $1,497 founding
- **Upsell to Construction Growth ($3,497):** ≥20% after month 3 (multi-crew operators)
