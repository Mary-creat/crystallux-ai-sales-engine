# Mary's Minimum Docs Package

**Purpose:** copy-ready business documents for Crystallux's first 30 days. All require one round of Canadian lawyer review before production use. All Canadian-context — CASL compliance, PIPEDA data handling, GST/HST tax treatment.

**Legal disclaimer:** the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy drafts below are starting points. They are **not** legal advice and **must** be reviewed by a Canadian business lawyer licensed in Mary's province of incorporation before use. Budget $500-1,500 for one-time review. Do not use Clerky or other US-template services without Canadian-specific amendments.

## 3. Privacy Policy (actual copy — PIPEDA-compliant, lawyer review required)

```
CRYSTALLUX PRIVACY POLICY

Effective Date: [DATE]
Last Updated: [DATE]

Crystallux Inc. ("Crystallux", "we", "us", or "our") is committed to
protecting your privacy and personal information in accordance with
Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act
(PIPEDA) and applicable provincial privacy laws.

1. WHAT WE COLLECT

We collect the following categories of personal information:

- FROM CLIENTS (businesses that subscribe to our platform): name,
  business name, business address, email, phone, payment information
  (stored by Stripe, not by us), Calendly link, logo (if provided),
  service-use analytics.
- FROM PROSPECTS (individuals whose contact information our clients
  surface via the platform): name, job title, employer, business
  address, business email, business phone, publicly available
  LinkedIn profile data, and publicly available firmographic data.

2. HOW WE COLLECT

- Directly from clients during signup and onboarding.
- From publicly available business directories, Apollo.io (B2B data
  provider), and publicly accessible social profiles (LinkedIn).
- From interactions with our platform (login events, dashboard use).

3. HOW WE USE

- Deliver the Crystallux Service to our clients.
- Send outreach on clients' behalf in compliance with CASL.
- Maintain service security, fraud prevention, and platform
  reliability.
- Comply with legal obligations (tax reporting, regulatory
  disclosures).

We do NOT sell personal information.

4. HOW WE SHARE

- With our clients, for the specific prospects they've requested
  outreach to.
- With service providers (Stripe for billing, Supabase for data
  hosting, Anthropic for AI content generation, Gmail / Twilio /
  Unipile / Vapi / Tavus for message delivery) bound by confidentiality.
- When required by Canadian law, court order, or regulatory request.

5. DATA STORAGE + SECURITY

Data stored in Supabase infrastructure in North America. TLS in
transit, encryption at rest, Row-Level Security on client-scoped
data. Access restricted to authorised Crystallux personnel.

6. YOUR RIGHTS (PIPEDA)

You have the right to:
- Know what personal information we hold about you.
- Correct inaccurate information.
- Request deletion (subject to legal retention obligations).
- Withdraw consent to processing (may terminate service eligibility).
- Escalate complaints to the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of
  Canada (priv.gc.ca).

To exercise rights: email privacy@crystallux.org with your request.
We respond within 30 days.

7. CASL COMPLIANCE

All commercial electronic messages sent through the Crystallux
platform comply with CASL. Clients warrant that any contacts uploaded
outside our sourced leads have CASL-compliant consent. Every outbound
message contains clear sender identification and an unsubscribe
mechanism.

8. COOKIES + ANALYTICS

Our dashboard uses session-only authentication tokens. We do not use
third-party advertising cookies. We use minimal analytics (Supabase
logs) for service reliability.

9. CHILDREN

Crystallux is a B2B service not intended for use by individuals under
18. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

10. INTERNATIONAL DATA

Some data may pass through US-based service providers (Stripe,
Anthropic). We ensure contractual data-protection obligations
consistent with PIPEDA when data leaves Canada.

11. CHANGES

We may update this Policy with 30 days' notice via email and
prominent dashboard notice.

12. CONTACT

Privacy Officer: privacy@crystallux.org
Mailing: [ADDRESS]
```

## 8. Onboarding Call Script (60-min first session with a new client)

```
INTRO (5 min)
"Welcome to Crystallux. Today's call is 60 minutes. We'll cover your
ICP, your first campaign setup, your dashboard access, and what to
expect over the next 7 days. Sound good?"

ICP DEEP-DIVE (20 min)
- Who are your 3 best customers from the last 12 months? Why?
- What's your ideal client size (revenue / employee count)?
- What service area do you serve — province, city, radius?
- Are you focused on residential, commercial, or both? (Sets
  clients.focus_segments)
- What's your deal size average? 12-month customer LTV?

CHANNEL SETUP (15 min)
- Which channels do you want live from day 1? (Sets
  clients.channels_enabled)
- Email: always on. Confirm your Gmail / business email for
  from-field.
- LinkedIn: do you have a LinkedIn account I can access via Unipile?
- SMS / WhatsApp: only if you have a Twilio number or we provision
  you one.
- Voice: deferred until Canadian DNCL real-check is live.
- Video: deferred until Tavus replica is trained — 24h lead time.

COPY REVIEW (10 min)
- I'll show you 3 sample outreach emails Claude would send to your
  ICP. You approve, flag, or edit.
- Your dental / legal / real-estate variant includes regulatory-
  compliance constraints. We flag anything that might breach.

DASHBOARD WALKTHROUGH (5 min)
- URL: crystallux.org/dashboard?client_id={YOUR_UUID}&token={YOUR_TOKEN}
- Bookmark it. Auto-refreshes every 60 seconds.
- You'll see pipeline health, leads, outreach, billing.

WHAT HAPPENS NEXT (5 min)
- Day 0 (today): this call, contract signed, Stripe provisioned,
  trial starts.
- Day 1-3: your first batch of 50 prospects loaded, pipeline runs.
- Day 4-7: first outreach sends, replies tracked, meetings booked.
- Day 14: first checkpoint call — review results, adjust ICP or copy.
- Day 30: first guarantee checkpoint (if vertical-specific target
  missed, month free).

ACTION ITEMS CAPTURED (on the call)
- Mary: configure client row, load first 50 prospects, dashboard
  URL delivered within 24h
- Client: approve sample outreach copy within 48h, forward Stripe
  welcome email to accounting if needed
- Both: book the day-14 checkpoint call on this call
```
