### Section headline

**If we miss the target, your next month is free.**

### The promise

Every vertical has a specific first-30-day target (listed above). If we don't hit it, your next month's invoice is waived. Not a partial credit. Not a future discount. Your card doesn't get charged.

- You have to actually use the product — meaning the channels we agreed on are on, the dashboard is being watched, and you're responding to the leads we surface.

## FAQ — pricing specific

### 1. What does "locked for 12 months" actually mean?

Your founding rate stays the same for 12 calendar months from the first day of your first paid month. Even when we raise the standard rate (we will), you keep your founding rate. After month 12, your rate auto-migrates to whatever the then-current standard rate is, unless you renew into a 12-month-locked upgrade tier.

### 2. Is there a setup fee?

No. Founding clients pay zero setup. Onboarding call, first campaign, dashboard, and credential setup are all included. We recover our cost from the recurring monthly.

### 3. How does billing work?

Stripe, monthly, in advance, in CAD. Plus applicable GST/HST based on your province. Card charged on day 1 of each paid month. Failed payment triggers a 7-day grace window (email + SMS reminders), after which the service pauses until the invoice is resolved.

### 4. Can I pause during a slow season?

Yes. Each founding contract includes one free pause per 12-month cycle: either a 30-day or 60-day pause. Your rate and channel configuration freeze during the pause. Beyond that, a paused month counts as a billed month.

### 5. What happens if I cancel mid-month?

Service continues through the current billing period; no refund for unused days. Your data (leads, outreach history, campaigns) is exported to CSV + JSON and delivered within 14 days of cancellation. After 90 days post-cancellation, personal data is deleted per our Privacy Policy.

### 6. Is there an annual discount?

Not yet. Founding month-to-month is already a 33-50% discount versus standard. We may introduce annual pre-pay discounts in Q3 2026; founding clients get first access.

### 7. What about taxes?

GST/HST is added on top of the listed price based on your province of business (Stripe Tax auto-calculates). If you're GST/HST-registered you can claim input tax credits in the normal way.

### 8. Can I change tiers mid-contract?

Yes. Upgrade any time — the new rate applies from the next billing cycle. Downgrade requires 30 days' notice and a brief conversation (we want to make sure we understand why).

## Cancellation + refund policy

- **Trial period (days 1-14 post-contract):** cancel any time during trial, no charge. Your data is deleted within 14 days unless you opt to convert to paid.
- **Post-trial monthly:** cancel with 30 days' written notice via info@crystallux.org. Final invoice is the current billing period only.
- **Guarantee remedy:** if we miss your first-30-day target, we waive the second month's invoice automatically. You don't have to ask.
- **Refunds:** we do not issue pro-rata refunds for mid-month cancellations. Credits against a future reactivation are available case-by-case.
- **Data retention:** 90 days post-cancellation, then deletion unless you request earlier.
