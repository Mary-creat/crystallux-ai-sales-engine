# Cold Outreach Templates — Mary Pitching Crystallux to Brokers

**Purpose:** Mary sends these (or adapts them per vertical) to pitch Crystallux. Peer-advisor voice, CASL-compliant, tight word counts.

**Compliance notes:**

- Every email includes Mary's business name, business address, and a working unsubscribe line in the footer.
- CASL implied-consent for business contacts at a public business email lasts 6 months; express consent is stronger — collect where possible.
- Do not batch-send these manually from Gmail. Use n8n or a CASL-compliant sender platform (Instantly, Lemlist) once volume exceeds 40/day.

**Target:** insurance brokers in Ontario. Adjust the specific pain hook per vertical using the pain_signals from the relevant `niche_overlays` row.

## Voicemail — 60-second script

Use only if mobile number is available and DNCL-compliant. Morning 8-9am before dispatch is the best window for contractors; noon-1pm for consultants; 4-5pm for brokers.

> "Hi [First Name], Mary Akintunde from Crystallux in Toronto. I'll keep this short — I work with [insurance brokers / contractors / etc.] in Ontario on outbound pipeline. Most operators I talk to are hitting the same wall: not enough hours in the week to cover the renewal / quoting / sales window manually.
>
> Crystallux is a done-for-you outbound engine for Canadian operators. Twenty qualified meetings a month, CASL-compliant, Canadian-built. If we don't hit 10 meetings in your first 30 days, the next month is free.
>
> Thirty seconds to see if it's worth a conversation: calendly.com/crystallux/discovery — or just reply to the email I sent yesterday.
>
> I'm at 416-***-**** if you want to call back. Thanks [First Name]."

**Read-time:** 55-58 seconds. Don't rush. The pause after "Toronto" is intentional — gives the name space to land.

## Master guardrails

Before any batch send:

- [ ] CASL footer present (business name, address, unsubscribe)
- [ ] Unsubscribe link works and processes within 10 business days per CASL
- [ ] Prospect has implied or express consent under CASL (public business contact + 6-month window minimum)
- [ ] Max 50 sends/day from any single Gmail account (deliverability hygiene)
- [ ] Mary's sending reputation checked monthly via MXToolbox or Google Postmaster
- [ ] "Remove" keyword in reply inbox triggers manual removal within 24h (until automated via n8n)

Violations trigger immediate pause-send and email to info@crystallux.org for review.
