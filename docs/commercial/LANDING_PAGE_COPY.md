## How it works

### Section headline

**Three steps. One engine. Your pipeline.**

### Step 1 — Discover

Crystallux identifies prospects who match your exact ideal client profile using Apollo, LinkedIn, Google Maps, and vertical-specific intent signals. Homeowners 60 days from renovating. Business owners up for insurance renewal. Real estate sellers pre-MLS. You define the filter once, we surface fresh matches every day.

### Step 2 — Enrich

Claude drafts research for every lead: what the business does, what they likely need, and the angle most likely to land. Every outreach is personalised to that lead specifically — no templates, no mail-merge, no "Hi [FIRST_NAME]."

### Step 3 — Engage

Multi-channel outreach sends on your behalf: email, LinkedIn, SMS, voice, video. Qualified replies get routed to your Calendly. Everything logs to your dashboard in real time. You see every send, every reply, every booking.

## FAQ

### Section headline

**Questions worth answering.**

### 1. How is this different from an SDR agency?

An SDR agency charges $3,000-5,000/month and you still write the sequences. An in-house BDR costs $8,000-12,000/month fully loaded. Crystallux runs the whole system — prospecting, copy, sending, booking — for $997-1,997/month. Canadian team. No sequences to write.

### 2. Do I have to write the outreach?

No. Claude authors every outreach email, LinkedIn message, SMS, voice script, and video script based on your vertical and your specific ICP. You approve the first batch during onboarding, then the system runs itself. You can edit copy any time.

### 3. What happens if I only get 5 meetings in 30 days?

Your second month is free. No partial credit, no future discount — your card doesn't get charged. After that, we keep iterating the copy and targeting until you hit the target.

### 4. Is this CASL-compliant?

Yes. Every outreach template is designed for CASL compliance. Each email includes sender identification, your business address, and a working unsubscribe. We maintain PIPEDA-aligned data handling and Canadian hosting.

### 5. How long does onboarding take?

You see your first outreach hit a prospect's inbox within 7 business days of contract signing. First qualified discovery calls typically booked in week 2. Full pipeline running by day 21.

### 6. What happens to my data if I cancel?

Your leads, campaigns, and outreach history are yours. We export everything to CSV + JSON within 14 days of cancellation. We retain anonymised aggregate metrics for benchmarking only.

### 7. Do I have to commit to a long contract?

No. Month-to-month after a 3-month initial term. Cancel with 30 days' notice. Founding pricing locked for 12 months if you stay.

### 8. What if I want to pause during a slow season?

Pausable. We offer 30-day and 60-day pauses once per 12-month cycle. Your founding rate and channel configuration freeze during the pause.

### 9. What's the difference between a Founding and Standard tier?

Price. Founding clients lock their rate for 12 months. Standard is the retail rate available after the founding window closes. Both get identical product.

### 10. Who owns the leads?

You do. Every lead Crystallux surfaces on your behalf is in your database, exportable, and yours to keep even after cancellation.
