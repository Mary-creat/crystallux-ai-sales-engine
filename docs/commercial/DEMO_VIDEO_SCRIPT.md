# Demo Video Script — 5-Minute Loom

**Purpose:** Mary records this once, keeps the URL, shares after every discovery call. Target length 5:00 ± 15 seconds. Tone: peer-to-peer, calm, no hype.

**Setup before recording:**

- Loom desktop app, Chrome in a private window at 1440×900
- Dashboard signed in as `admin=true` with demo data loaded (see `docs/operations/DEMO_DATA_PREP.md` — seed with 25 synthetic leads + 2 demo clients)
- Second monitor with this script scrolling
- Camera on (bubble in corner, bottom-left)
- Mic: good external mic or AirPods Pro, quiet room
- Record in HD, 24fps

**Export:** upload to Loom, password-protect the video (password: `founding`), enable download. Embed on pricing page + landing page + attach to post-demo email.

---

## 0:00 — 0:30 · HOOK

[Camera on Mary, dashboard just visible]

> "Hey, Mary from Crystallux. I'm going to show you what happens in the 20 minutes after you sign up — because the discovery call you just had was honest, but a demo is always clearer than a deck. Five minutes, no fluff."

[Cut to full-screen dashboard]

> "If you're a Canadian broker, contractor, consultant, or service business owner, the problem I hear every week is the same: your pipeline only moves when you personally move it. Weekends disappear into quoting. Bizdev eats the hours that should go to delivery. Outreach at scale sounds like outreach at scale."

> "Crystallux fixes that. Here's what it actually looks like."

**[Tone note: calm, steady, slight under-the-breath confidence. Don't rush the first 30 seconds.]**

---

## 0:30 — 1:15 · DASHBOARD TOUR

**[SHARE SCREEN 1: dashboard overview — pipeline stats + channels active panel + billing panel]**

> "This is the dashboard every client gets. One URL, one token, your whole pipeline."

[Point with cursor to pipeline stats tile]

> "Pipeline health at the top. Leads in, outreach sent, replies received, meetings booked — auto-refresh every 60 seconds."

[Scroll to Channels Active panel]

> "Channels Active tells you which outreach methods are live for your business. Email always on. LinkedIn turns on when we've wired up Unipile together. Video turns on when your Tavus replica is trained. This is how you see what's configured."

[Scroll to billing panel]

> "Billing panel — your subscription status, plan, next billing date, last payment. Manage Billing opens the Stripe customer portal. Zero surprises, zero admin."

**[Tone note: relaxed, pointing with cursor. Don't speed through — let viewers read tile labels.]**

---

## 1:15 — 2:30 · LEAD GENERATION FLOW

**[SHARE SCREEN 2: leads grid with demo leads]**

> "Here's a lead. Every lead carries research summary, lead score, detected signal, campaign assignment, preferred channel, and the channels already attempted."

[Click one lead, show detail]

> "Click any lead and you see the full history. Every email sent, every LinkedIn touch, every reply parsed by Claude, every status change. Full audit trail."

**[SHARE SCREEN 3: outreach generation log or generation prompt preview]**

> "Under the hood, every outreach is Claude-authored. Not templated. Not mail-merged. Claude reads your vertical's playbook, the specific lead's research, the signal we detected, and writes peer-advisor copy tuned to that lead."

> "That's why early clients told us the outreach reads like a person reached out, not a vendor. An insurance broker rated a recent outbound email 9.5 out of 10 — said it was the first cold touch that felt like a peer."

**[Tone note: light pride, no hype. Let the Filip quote sit for half a beat.]**

---

## 2:30 — 3:30 · LIVE EMAIL EXAMPLE

**[SHARE SCREEN 4: outreach_log panel with a specific sent email expanded]**

> "Here's an actual email that went out yesterday, anonymised. Fifteen-second read, then I'll tell you why it works."

[Pause 15 seconds while the email is on-screen. Viewer reads.]

> "Three things making this work:"

> "One — the opener references a real, researched detail. Not 'Hi [First Name]' — an actual observation from that business's website."

> "Two — the pain hook is niche-specific. For insurance brokers it's renewal season. For contractors it's Google Ads burning through $60-per-lead tire-kickers. We tune the hook to the vertical."

> "Three — the ask is small. Twenty minutes, no deck, no pressure. That's the conversation starter that converts at 8-15%, versus 1-3% for generic cold outreach."

**[Tone note: instructional but peer-level. You're showing, not selling.]**

---

## 3:30 — 4:15 · PRICING + GUARANTEE

**[SHARE SCREEN 5: pricing page on crystallux.org]**

> "Pricing is simple. Four active verticals, three tiers."

> "Moving and cleaning: $997 a month. Construction, real estate, and dental: $1,497. Insurance brokers and consulting: $1,997. Canadian dollars, plus GST. Monthly, via Stripe. Cancel any time."

> "Founding pricing locked for 12 months for the first 20 clients per vertical. Right now you qualify. After the founding window closes, the rate goes up."

> "And the guarantee: every vertical has a first-30-day target. For insurance brokers, it's 10 qualified meetings. Miss that, the next month is free. Not a partial credit, not a future discount — your card doesn't get charged. We eat the risk because we're confident in the system."

**[Tone note: flat, factual. Don't over-sell the guarantee; let the promise speak.]**

---

## 4:15 — 5:00 · CTA

**[Back to camera, dashboard visible in background]**

> "Two paths from here. Path one — you're in. Reply to the demo email, I'll send a contract, we kick off onboarding tomorrow, your first outreach hits a prospect's inbox by end of week one. Your founding rate locks for 12 months."

> "Path two — you want to think. Totally fair. I'll send a one-pager you can share with your partner or your broker of record. Then reconnect next week — I'll check in Thursday if we haven't spoken."

> "Either way, thanks for watching. Five minutes well spent for both of us if we work together, or five minutes well spent if we don't. Talk soon."

[Camera hold 2 seconds, then cut]

**[Tone note: warm close. No forced "smash subscribe" energy. Exit clean.]**
