## Engagement strategy

Rank and comment on specific people's content daily. Goal: 10-15 quality comments per day on target ICP's posts.

### Whose content to engage with

- **Canadian insurance brokers' LinkedIn posts** — especially RIBO-licensed independent brokers in Ontario
- **Contractors posting job-site content** — especially 5-20 employee GCs in the GTA
- **Real estate agents' market commentary** — ignore listing posts, engage on market analysis
- **Dental practice owners** — often post about practice-management topics
- **Founder-voice Canadian SMB content** — founders sharing honest operator stories

### What to comment

- **Add a specific observation.** "This matches exactly what I'm seeing with brokers in the GTA — renewal season just keeps narrowing." Better than "Great post!"
- **Ask a pointed question.** "Curious: when you say 'marketing cost', are you including the time you spend writing proposals?" Better than generic agreement.
- **Share a small insight without pitching.** "One thing we've seen work for brokers in this pattern: scheduling outreach 60 days before renewal instead of 30. Different window, different receptivity."
- **Never paste the Crystallux pitch.** That's what the DM is for.

### What NOT to do

- Don't post daily company-page content just to post. 2-3 posts per week is the ceiling.
- Don't comment "DM me" or "link in bio" — feels scammy.
- Don't engage in political or divisive content from any angle, even if invited.
- Don't auto-like everything. LinkedIn's algorithm penalises low-quality engagement.

### Cadence

- **Morning (30 min):** check notifications, reply to comments on own posts, DM 3-5 warm prospects per message templates in `COLD_OUTREACH_TEMPLATES.md`
- **Afternoon (20 min):** scroll target audience feeds, add 10-15 genuine comments
- **Evening (0 min):** off
