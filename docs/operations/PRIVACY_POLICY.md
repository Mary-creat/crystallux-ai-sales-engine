## 3. Purposes for collection

Crystallux collects and uses personal information for the following purposes:

### For Clients (businesses that subscribe to Crystallux)

- Account creation and service delivery
- Billing and payment processing via Stripe
- Customer support and communications
- Compliance with legal and regulatory obligations
- Marketing (with your consent)

### For Leads (individuals whose contact information our clients surface via the platform)

- Delivering outreach on behalf of our clients, in compliance with CASL
- Personalising outreach based on publicly available business information
- Recording replies and managing follow-up sequences
- Enabling meeting-booking automation
- Maintaining audit logs for legal and compliance purposes

We do **not** collect personal information for purposes beyond those listed above without further consent.

## 4. Consent

### How consent is obtained

**For Clients:** consent is obtained through the Master Services Agreement, which explicitly references this Privacy Policy, combined with acceptance of Terms of Service on account creation.

**For Leads:** our clients are responsible for ensuring that the contacts they engage with through Crystallux have provided appropriate **CASL-compliant consent** (implied or express). Crystallux sources leads from publicly available business information (Apollo, LinkedIn business profiles, public directories, Google Maps) where implied consent for business-to-business communication is typically available under CASL.

Every outreach message sent through Crystallux includes:
- Clear sender identification (client's business name)
- Client's business contact information
- A working unsubscribe mechanism

### How consent is withdrawn

- **Clients** may withdraw consent by terminating the MSA per clause 9 of the agreement
- **Leads** may withdraw consent at any time by replying to any Crystallux-sourced outreach with "unsubscribe", "remove", or equivalent language — we process such requests within 10 business days per CASL

Withdrawal of consent may limit our ability to provide the Service.

## 5. What personal information is collected

### From Clients (businesses)

- Full name, business name, and business address
- Business email and phone number
- Business Number (BN), HST/GST registration, and Stripe billing details
- Calendly URL or equivalent booking URL
- Logo and brand assets (if provided)

### From Leads (individuals whose contact information our clients surface)

- Full name, job title, and employer name
- Business email and business phone number (work contacts only)
- Business address and city of operation
- Publicly available LinkedIn profile data (headline, industry, mutual connections)
- Publicly available firmographic data (company size, revenue range, industry)
- Reply content from outreach exchanges (for routing to the client's calendar or inbox)
- Meeting booking metadata (if a booking is made via Calendly)

### What we do NOT collect

- Payment information from leads (we never process lead payments)
- Sensitive personal information (health, biometric, financial account numbers for non-clients)
- Government identifiers (SIN, driver's licence, passport)
- Minors' data (service is not intended for use by anyone under 18)

## 9. Data security

We implement technical, organisational, and physical safeguards to protect personal information:

- **Encryption in transit:** TLS 1.3 for all connections to Crystallux and its service providers
- **Encryption at rest:** Supabase database encryption at rest
- **Access controls:** Row-Level Security (RLS) on client-scoped data in Supabase; authentication via time-limited dashboard tokens; multi-factor authentication on all Crystallux admin accounts
- **Audit logs:** we maintain logs of all data access and modifications in Supabase
- **Personnel practices:** Crystallux personnel receive privacy and security training; access to production data is limited to what is necessary for the role

No system is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we follow industry standards and will notify you of any breach that materially affects your personal information.

## 10. Data retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes identified:

- **Client data (during active term):** retained for the duration of the MSA
- **Client data (post-termination):** retained for 90 days, then deleted
- **Lead data (active clients):** retained for the duration of the client's active term
- **Lead data (after client terminates):** retained for 90 days, then deleted
- **Financial records:** retained for 7 years as required by Canadian tax law (CRA)
- **Outreach logs:** retained for 3 years post-client-termination for compliance and dispute-resolution purposes, then deleted

If you wish data to be deleted earlier, see clause 11.

## 11. Your rights under PIPEDA

Under PIPEDA, you have the right to:

### 11.1 Right to access

You may request confirmation of whether Crystallux holds personal information about you and, if so, receive a copy. We respond within 30 days.

### 11.2 Right to correct

You may request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal information. We respond within 30 days and either make the correction or explain why we will not.

### 11.3 Right to withdraw consent

You may withdraw consent for the collection, use, or disclosure of your personal information at any time, subject to legal or contractual restrictions and reasonable notice. Withdrawal may limit our ability to continue providing the Service.

### 11.4 Right to challenge compliance

You may challenge Crystallux's compliance with PIPEDA by contacting our Privacy Officer. If unsatisfied with our response, you may escalate to the **Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada**:

- **Web:** priv.gc.ca
- **Phone:** 1-800-282-1376
- **Mail:** 30 Victoria Street, Gatineau, Québec K1A 1H3

### 11.5 How to exercise these rights

Email **info@crystallux.org** with subject line "Privacy — [request type]". Include:

- Your name and business
- The specific right you are exercising
- Sufficient detail to locate the relevant information

We respond within **30 days** as required by PIPEDA.

## 15. Quebec residents (Law 25 addendum)

If you are a resident of Quebec, additional rights apply under Quebec's **Loi modernisant des dispositions législatives en matière de protection des renseignements personnels (Law 25)**:

- **Data portability:** right to request your personal information in a structured, commonly used format
- **Right to decommissioning:** right to request removal or de-indexing of your personal information in specific circumstances
- **Automated decision-making disclosure:** where automated decisions are made about you, you have the right to know

To exercise Quebec-specific rights, email info@crystallux.org with "Quebec — Law 25 request" in the subject line.
